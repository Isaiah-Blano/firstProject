This is my tutorial on how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

More Recipes Using Peanut Butter and Jelly

Ingredients:
  • Bread (pretty much any kind of bread will do)
  • Jelly/Jam (preferrably grape.)
  • Peanut Butter

Step 1

First, spread a half teaspoon of butter on each slice of bread.

Step 2

Swirl jam into peanut butter.

Step 3

Cover with other slice of bread.
